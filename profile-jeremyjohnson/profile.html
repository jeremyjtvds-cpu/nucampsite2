About

Last August, my son, Jack, asked if we could go on a backpacking trip in the Sawtooth Mountains for his 12th birthday.
So we set off on our trek to Sawtooth Lake with our dogs, Maggie and Rocky. It was a very scenic, beautiful hike.
It was Jack's first time carrying all of his own gear, so he got tuckered out pretty good.
We didn't make it all the way to Sawtooth Lake. We stopped at Alpine Lake and camped there instead.
I had been to Sawtooth Lake before, so I was happy to experience a brand new place for the first time with my son.
